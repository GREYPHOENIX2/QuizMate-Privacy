Privacy Policy – QuizMate: Life Sciences Gr12

QuizMate : Life Sciences Gr12 provides interactive quizzes for Grade 12 Life Sciences learners in South Africa. Our app helps students practice and assess their knowledge for educational purposes.

Information We Collect

We use Google’s AdMob advertising platform, which may collect your device’s advertising ID to deliver personalized or non-personalized ads. QuizMate : Life Sciences Gr12 itself does not collect any personally identifiable information from users.

How We Use Information

The advertising ID is used to display relevant ads and measure ad performance. We may also use aggregated analytics data from Google services to monitor app performance and improve functionality.

Data Sharing

The advertising ID is shared with Google AdMob for the purpose of serving ads and analyzing ad performance. No other user data is shared with third parties.

Children

This app is intended for Grade 12 learners and is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

User Rights

Users can reset or limit their device advertising ID in Android settings to opt out of personalized ads.

Security

All user interactions and quiz answers are stored locally on your device. No personal data is transmitted or stored on external servers by the app itself.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page. Please check periodically for updates.
